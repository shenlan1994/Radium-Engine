Detailed Description

Structure holding the half-edge data of one mesh.

Note on non manifold edges : if the mesh is non-closed, two half edges will be constructed even on the border edges, however the "outer" half-edge will have invalid face and prev/next indices. Edges shared by more than 2 triangles are not supported and will cause an assert during the call to build().
